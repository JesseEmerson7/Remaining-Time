To calculate the time required to clock out:

  • Enter the total number of hours you have worked so far after lunch on Friday (e.g., 37.23).
  • The system calculates the remaining hours to reach 40 hours by subtracting the entered hours from 40.
  • The remaining decimals are converted to minutes by multiplying by 60.
  • The system then determines how many hours and minutes are left in the day based on the remaining time.
  • For example, if you enter 37.23 hours:
    • 40 - 37.23 = 2.77 hours remaining
    • .77 Ã— 60 = 46.2 minutes
    • The clock-out time will be your clock-in time plus 2 hours and 46 minutes.
